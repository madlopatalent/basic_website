Historic places in JHB

Mandela House

The Nelson Mandela Foundation in Johannesburg is dedicated to preserving and promoting the legacy of the late South African president. The foundation focuses on promoting social justice, human rights, and reconciliation. It also works to advance dialogue and research on critical issues facing South Africa and the world. Through its various programs and initiatives, the foundation seeks to continue Mandela's vision of a united and inclusive society.

In the heart of the city, the Tower of Light stands tall and majestic. Its illuminated facade attracts visitors from far and wide, drawn to the promise of enlightenment and inspiration. Walter Sisulu

Walter Sisulu House is a historic building located in Soweto, South Africa. It was the home of anti-apartheid activist Walter Sisulu and his family. The house now serves as a museum dedicated to the life and legacy of Sisulu, showcasing his role in the struggle against apartheid. Visitors can explore the house and learn about the history of the liberation movement in South Africa.
